Generative AI Cover Maker – Full Project

Description & Motivation

The Generative AI Cover Maker is a creative tool designed to automatically generate playlist cover art based on mood, color, or theme inputs. People often attach emotion and identity to playlists, but most never customize the artwork because it requires design skill or time.

Our goal was to make artistic expression effortless — letting users transform music taste into visuals in seconds through AI. With generative AI becoming more accessible, this project explores how users interact with creative AI tools, how interface choices influence trust and understanding, and how customization features impact satisfaction.

Online Study

Our initial online study gathered expectations around playlist cover aesthetics and perceptions of AI design. Participants viewed sample imagery and answered questions on what feels meaningful versus random. Most users wanted covers that visually match playlist themes and requested customization controls such as color mood, style, and layout adjustments.

This confirmed that AI should produce quick results but still allow human input, leading us to design a workflow where users generate first, then personalize.

Prototyping Phase

We designed two low-fidelity prototypes:

  • Design A – Step-by-Step Guided Flow: Connect → Generate → Customize → Download
  • Design B – Dashboard Layout: All tools visible at once

Participants used printed/digital mockups and completed tasks like connecting a playlist, generating a cover, and customizing it. The main goal was to compare comfort, clarity, and creativity between workflows.

User Studies Results

User Study 2 – Functional Prototype Evaluation

Users tested a working version: connect playlist → generate cover → customize → download. Sessions captured confusion points and behavior patterns.

What worked well:

  • Smooth playlist connection
  • AI generation was exciting & matched playlist vibe
  • Downloading was clear and quick

Challenges:

  • Customization UI caused confusion
  • Buttons not clearly labeled
  • Users expected more styling options or draggable elements

Next Iterations:

  • Guide customization more clearly with labels/tooltips
  • Improve visibility of styling features
  • Offer mood/color presets

Overall, the main interaction flow is intuitive and enjoyable. Enhancing clarity in the customization phase will improve user confidence and creativity.
